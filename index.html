Company Profile

Arabian Bureau of Services

established on 1993 as a 3rd party inspection company for the thorough examination of Lifting Appliances, Lifting Gears, Earthmoving Machineries, Industrial Trucks & QHSE training and consultancy. During this period ABS had grown and expanded in the U.A.E market and established a high strength relationship among its customers whose trust by its judgment. Our judgment against any equipment, machine or accessory gear gives priority of preventing equipment, machine, accessory gear, operators, labors, environment and the property from any accident, deterioration, damage, harm, fatal injuries or death. For more than two decade of operations, ABS has gained much experience in the UAE and has preserved its commitment to provide quality inspection service to all our clients. Qualified and experienced staffs were retained to ensure a continuity of quality inspection & training services.

ABS’s main core businesses are in the following items

  1. Inspection, testing and examination of lifting appliances and lifting gears.
  2. Inspection, testing and examination of Earth moving machinery, Industrial.
  3. Examination the competency of the above mentioned equipment and machines operators and conducting safe use of the equipment training courses for them.
  4. Conducting safety & Vocational training courses.

ABS’s is doing consultation on Quality Management Systems, Environmental Management System and Safety Management System training & Consultancy.
